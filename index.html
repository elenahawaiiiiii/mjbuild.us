About Us



M&J Construction Inc. is a premier General Contractor based in New York City, specializing in commercial and mixed-use residential projects. With a proven track record in delivering high-quality developments, we provide a full spectrum of construction services, including Construction Management (CM), interior fit-out, and HVAC system installation.

With over a billion dollars in total project value, our expertise spans large-scale developments, ensuring seamless execution from pre-construction planning to final delivery. We collaborate closely with developers, architects, and engineers to meet the highest industry standards while adhering to budget and timeline

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

To be the industry leader in building sustainable and innovative spaces.
